About the Orchestra

The Westminster Philharmonic Orchestra is an amateur orchestra based in central London, performing a full symphonic repertoire. We welcome musicians of all backgrounds who share a love of orchestral music.

We perform two or three concerts a year, typically at St John's Smith Square, and rehearse weekly throughout the season.
